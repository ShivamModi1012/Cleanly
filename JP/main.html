      You’ve got questions?

      We’ve got answers

      How do I schedule my first Rinse?

      Once you’ve created your account you can schedule online or via our mobile app (available on iOS and Android) You can also text “Y” to 746-73 (CLEANLY) before 5pm for a pickup that same evening.

      Can I receive a delivery if I’m not home?

      No problem! We can pick up and drop off right from your doorstep or concierge. Email [email protected] to set your “personless” preferences.

      How do I get my Cleanly bags?

      Your Valet will bring your personalized Cleanly bags to your first pickup. They’re yours to use for all future Rinse orders!
